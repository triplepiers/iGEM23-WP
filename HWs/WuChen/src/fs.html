My favorite singer: Lana Del Rey

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant (born June 21, 1985), known professionally as Lana Del Rey, is an American singer-songwriter. Her music is noted for its cinematic quality and exploration of tragic romance, glamour, and melancholia, with frequent references to contemporary pop culture and 1950s–70s Americana.

第一次听打雷姐的歌是在初中，那时正值青春成长阵痛期，突然听《young and beautiful》，实在是喜欢的不得了，可以说这首歌是我的入门曲。打雷姐的歌里仿佛都带着神性，曼妙歌声能让灵魂超脱凡俗直抵天堂，陪伴我度过敏感孤独的成长期。
