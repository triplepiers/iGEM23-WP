High blood sugar

spells disaster

Excessive blood glucose leads to the production of advanced glycation end products(AGEs) and leads to enhanced inflammation and deficiency of growth factors, further worsening the wound.

    In our project GluLeaves, engineered Chlamydomonas reinhardtii cells would release synthetic proteins in a specially formulated dressing to block the wound healing difficulties associated with high blood sugar while fighting bacterial infection.

    Also, when the wound infection worsens, the dressing will turn red to alert the user to seek medical attention.
